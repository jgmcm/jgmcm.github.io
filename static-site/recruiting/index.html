Recruiting

Information for prospective PhD, postdoctoral and visiting researchers.

I do not currently have an advertised funded PhD position.

I am, however, happy to hear from strong prospective students who would like to develop an application with me for competitive funding at the University of Manchester. These schemes need preparation, so please make contact well before the relevant deadline.

Current route

Applying for competitive funding

Send a concise email with your CV, relevant computational or theoretical experience, and the research theme you would like to explore. A specific question is more useful than a generic request for a position.

What I am looking for

Prospective PhD students

Candidates interested in first-principles modelling, moiré materials, ferroelectrics, magnetism or atomistic machine learning—and prepared to apply for competitive funding.

Postdoctoral fellows

I am always keen to hear from researchers with overlapping interests. Please email a CV and a short statement.

If your interests overlap with the group, send a short note about your background and the problem you would like to work on.

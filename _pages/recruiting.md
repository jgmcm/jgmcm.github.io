#I am recruiting PhD students, post-doctoral researchers, research assistants, and visiting students 

---
Since January 2025, I have been a Dame Kathleen Ollerenshaw Research Fellow at the University of Manchester. My group works on theoretical, computational, ab initio, and machine-learning modelling of complex electron-density effects in two-dimensional materials.

<div class="opportunity-panel">
  <h2>Current PhD Projects</h2>

  <article>
    <h3><a href="https://www.findaphd.com/phds/project/neural-quantum-states-for-automated-discovery-of-emergent-quantum-phases-in-two-dimensional-materials/?p196498">Neural Quantum States for Automated Discovery of Emergent Quantum Phases in Two-Dimensional Materials</a></h3>
    <p>A funded PhD project using neural quantum state methods to automate the identification of emergent correlated phases in 2D materials and moiré systems.</p>
  </article>

  <article>
    <h3><a href="https://www.findaphd.com/phds/project/multi-modal-ai-models-for-designer-ferroelectric-devices/?p191593">Multi-modal AI models for designer ferroelectric devices</a></h3>
    <p>EPSRC CDT in Materials 4.0 / Henry Royce Institute. This fully funded project investigates multilayer functional devices built from stacked 2D semiconductors and magnetic materials.</p>
  </article>
</div>
